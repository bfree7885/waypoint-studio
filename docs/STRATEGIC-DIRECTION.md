# Waypoint Studio — Strategic Direction

**Status:** Active · Post Expert Council & Portfolio Audit refactor  
**Scope:** Pike County Preview until county-specific bundles and observation capture ship

---

## What we are

Waypoint Studio is **not an eight-app company**.

It is an **outdoor intelligence and environmental education platform** with one regional trailhead and three specialized instruments:

| Surface | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Waypoint Studio Dashboard** | Regional outdoor intelligence — what is happening, what to learn, what to observe, how to go outside ethically |
| **ForageCast** | Flagship application — habitat literacy, seasonal timing, ethical field investigation |
| **Fieldry** | Observation ledger — structured field records building toward research-grade capture (WOS) |
| **Waypoint Scenes** | Creative reflection — honest field photography and visual storytelling |

**Platform foundations** (OIP, WOS, WEF, ethics, research integrity) are not public products. They are shared infrastructure.

---

## Mission pillars

Every feature must support at least one pillar; ideally all three:

1. **Learn** about the natural world  
2. **Get outdoors**  
3. **Contribute toward research-grade environmental observations** (building over time)

Honest language:

- ✅ “Designed to support research-grade environmental observations”  
- ✅ “Building toward research-grade observation tools”  
- ❌ “Research-grade data pipeline” (until capture ships)  
- ❌ National-scale outdoor intelligence (until per-county bundles exist)

---

## What we are not building as standalone products

| Former concept | New status |
|----------------|------------|
| Shed Hunting | Winter wildlife / deer ecology **content track** |
| Steepleaf | Botany & phenology **track** inside ForageCast Learn |
| SignalTerrain | Shared **map / navigation / earth science modules** |
| Savant Sommelier | Occasional **landscape & terroir editorial** |
| Terrainbound | **Retired** — route notes → Fieldry; trail conditions → Dashboard |

Concepts remain in the repository. Public marketing presents **four core instruments**, not eight equal doors.

---

## Geographic honesty

**Pike County Preview** is the current regional model.

- Label: “Pike County Preview” on dashboard and flagship apps  
- Other locations may use nearest editorial bundle — disclose honestly  
- Do not imply national coverage until county-specific intelligence exists

---

## Investment order

1. **Dashboard** — progressive learning path, render unshown educational JSON, Pike scope labels  
2. **Fieldry + WOS local capture** — unlock observation moat  
3. **ForageCast depth** — species, habitat, ethical investigation copy  
4. **Scenes v0.1.0** — ship and freeze scope  
5. **Content tracks** — seasonal modules, not new app shells
