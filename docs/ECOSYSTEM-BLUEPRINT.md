# Waypoint Studio — Master Experience Blueprint

> **Strategic note (2026):** The seven-room pattern remains the **content architecture** for lessons and editorial pages. The **public product portfolio** is four instruments — Dashboard, ForageCast, Fieldry, Scenes. Former app concepts (Shed Hunting, Steepleaf, etc.) become content tracks or shared modules. See [STRATEGIC-DIRECTION.md](STRATEGIC-DIRECTION.md).

**Mission:** Observe · Understand · Create · Share

**What we are:** A place people visit because they love nature — not a collection of utilities.

**What we are not:** Social media, a startup dashboard, enterprise software.

**The feeling:** Warm. Cozy. Traditional. Handcrafted. Educational. Quiet. Thoughtful.  
Like a mountain cabin after a day outside. Like a Peterson Field Guide. Like National Geographic, Cornell Lab, PBS Nature, a state park visitor center, a nature museum.

---

## The Seven Rooms

Every Waypoint Studio product is a **destination** with the same seven rooms. Visitors may enter through any door, but the house always feels familiar.

```
                    ┌─────────────┐
                    │    HOME     │  ← Arrival. Season. Story. Invitation.
                    └──────┬──────┘
           ┌───────────────┼───────────────┐
           ▼               ▼               ▼
      ┌─────────┐    ┌──────────┐    ┌──────────┐
      │  LEARN  │◄──►│ GALLERY  │◄──►│  FIELD   │
      │ (heart) │    │          │    │  NOTES   │
      └────┬────┘    └────┬─────┘    └────┬─────┘
           │              │               │
           └──────┬───────┴───────┬───────┘
                  ▼               ▼
            ┌─────────┐     ┌─────────┐
            │  NEWS   │     │ VIDEOS  │
            └────┬────┘     └────┬────┘
                 │               │
                 └───────┬───────┘
                         ▼
                   ┌──────────┐
                   │  TOOLS   │  ← The workshop. Never the front door.
                   └──────────┘
```

**Navigation principle:** Primary nav reads like a field station signpost — *Home · Learn · Gallery · Field Notes · News · Videos · Tools* — not product features.

**Content principle:** A visitor should want to stay an hour without opening Tools. Photography, illustration, maps, and story lead. Software serves the story.

## Shared section anatomy

### HOME
- Full-bleed hero photograph (changes with season)
- One-line seasonal message — handwritten in tone, not marketing
- Featured story (essay, dispatch, or observation)
- Today's lesson (one WEF lesson, linked to Learn)
- Recent observations (from Field Notes, 3 cards)
- Featured video (thumbnail only; never autoplay)
- **Explore** — soft CTA into Learn or Gallery, not "Get started"

### LEARN *(the heart)*
Structured like a field guide library, not a course catalog.

| Track | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **101** | First visit — how to read this place |
| **102** | Going deeper — habits of observation |
| **Species** | Who lives here |
| **Habitats** | Where life happens |
| **Ecology** | How it connects |
| **Weather** | Sky and season |
| **Geology** | Stone and time |
| **Photography** | Seeing clearly |
| **Field Skills** | Hands in the field |
| **Conservation** | Care and responsibility |

Each lesson: illustration or diagram · map where relevant · related lessons · suggested reading order · field exercise · ethics · safety · challenge.

### GALLERY
- Large photography, minimal chrome
- Every frame: story · camera settings · field note · location · season
- Links: related species · related lesson
- Actions (quiet, secondary): *Create Living Scene* · *Feel Depth* (Scenes only)

### FIELD NOTES
- Journal entries, sketches, observations
- Seasonal change logs
- Research notes (curated, editorial — not a social feed)
- Hand-drawn or watercolor illustration where possible

### NEWS
- Editorial desk, not an algorithm
- Conservation · research · season · migration · weather · park updates · astronomy · natural events
- Dated dispatches; archive by season

### VIDEOS
- Curated shelves: beginner series · how-to · ecology · photography
- Thumbnail + title + duration; click to play
- **Never autoplay**

### TOOLS
- The application workshop — prediction, animation, mapping, logging, search, ID
- Reachable from nav, never the homepage hero
- Copy: *"When you're ready to work with today's frame…"*

## Waypoint Scenes

**Role:** The studio by the fire — where still photographs breathe.

**Visitor promise:** *You made it home. Let the day breathe.*

### Homepage
- Hero: mist, ridge, or firelight — user or curated field frame
- Seasonal message: *"Late light lingers longer now."*
- Featured story: *"Why motion should honor the frame"* (essay)
- Today's lesson: from Photography or Field Skills track
- Recent observations: last three unpacked frames (Field Notes)
- Featured video: *"Reading light in a forest"* (5 min, no autoplay)
- Explore → Gallery *"Back from the field"*

### Learn
- **101:** Unpacking your first frame
- **102:** Mood vs. mask — when motion serves the photograph
- **Photography:** light, composition, weather in frame
- **Weather / Ecology:** atmosphere in scene (fog, rain, fireflies as natural phenomena)
- **Field Skills:** patience, field ethics, leaving no trace with a camera
- **Conservation:** honest representation — don't fake wildlife behavior
- Illustrations: layer diagrams (foreground / sky / atmosphere)
- Maps: optional — location of featured gallery frames

### Gallery
- *Collections* — masonry of field returns
- Plaque copy: settings, field note, season, place
- Related lesson: *"Morning Mist preset and real fog"*
- Quiet actions: Bring to life · Feel depth

### Field Notes
- Per-frame journal: what you heard, smelled, waited for
- Seasonal series: *"Same trail, four seasons"*
- Sketches: composition thumbnails (illustrated)

### News
- *"On the horizon"* — only honest roadmap items as natural-history dispatches
- Seasonal photography notes: equinox light, migration backdrop
- Conservation: dark sky, wildlife distance guidelines

### Videos
- *Gentle drift* — what each atmosphere layer does
- Beginner: upload, one preset, export one still
- No autoplay; fireplace pacing

### Tools
- **Living Scene** — atmosphere and time
- **Parallax** — presence and depth
- **Export** — share one still moment
- Nav label: *Studio* or *Workbench*, not feature names on Home

**Connections:** Home → today's lesson in Learn. Gallery frame → Field Note → Lesson on light. News "on the horizon" never competes with Home hero. Tools only after visitor has seen Gallery or Learn.

## Cross-product connections

### The reading paths

| Path | Journey |
|------|---------|
| **Observer** | Home → Learn 101 → Field Notes → Gallery |
| **Student** | Learn track → Videos → Field exercise → Field Notes |
| **Maker** | Gallery → Tools (Scenes: Living Scene) → Export → Field Notes reflection |
| **Citizen** | News → Learn Conservation → Field Notes → share ethically |

### Sister links (subtle, not promotional)

| From | To | Link tone |
|------|-----|-----------|
| Scenes Gallery | Learn Photography | *"How this light works"* |
| ForageCast Species | Steepleaf Species | *"Same forest, different key"* |
| Fieldry Navigation lesson | SignalTerrain Learn | *"Continue with maps"* |
| SignalTerrain Map | Terrainbound Routes | *"Walk this contour"* |
| Savant Sommelier Ecology | Steepleaf Habitat | *"What grows on this slope"* |
| Any product | Waypoint Studio Home | *"More places to explore"* |

### WEF (Learn engine) across products

Same eleven-part lesson template everywhere. Product accent changes; structure does not. **101** and **102** are mandatory in every product. Suggested reading order appears at the top of Learn like a field guide introduction.

### Visual language

- Photography: large, slow to load intentionally if needed — quality over speed
- Illustration: watercolor, ink, Peterson-style plates — not clip art
- Maps: restrained palette, serif labels, north arrow, scale bar
- Typography: display serif for titles; quiet sans for metadata
- Motion: only in Tools (Scenes) and Video (on click)
- No feeds, likes, counts, or infinite scroll on Home

## Homepage hierarchy (never violate)

1. **Photograph** — nature leads
2. **Seasonal sentence** — human voice
3. **Story** — why care today
4. **Today's lesson** — one teachable thing
5. **Recent observations** — proof of life
6. **Featured video** — invitation, not autoplay
7. **Explore** — into Learn or Gallery
8. **Tools** — in nav only; never item 1–3

---

## What we will not do

- Autoplay video or motion on arrival
- Engagement metrics visible to visitors
- Feature-first heroes ("Upload now!")
- Dark patterns toward Tools
- Social feeds, comments, or rankings
- Startup jargon ("unlock", "optimize", "dashboard")
- Shipping software without a lesson beside it
