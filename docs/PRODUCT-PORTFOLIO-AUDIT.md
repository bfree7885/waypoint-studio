# Waypoint Studio — Product Portfolio Audit

**Date:** Strategic refactor pass  
**Mission lenses:** Learn · Get outdoors · Research-grade observations

---

## Executive summary

Waypoint Studio becomes stronger as **one regional laboratory with four instruments**, not eight equal apps.

| Verdict | Products |
|---------|----------|
| **Essential** | Dashboard, ForageCast, Fieldry |
| **Important** | Waypoint Scenes |
| **Postpone** | Shed Hunting, Steepleaf, SignalTerrain, Savant Sommelier (as products) |
| **Remove** | Terrainbound (as standalone product) |

---

## Core portfolio

### Waypoint Studio Dashboard — **Essential**

**Role:** Regional trailhead and outdoor intelligence surface.

| Pillar | Fit |
|--------|-----|
| Learn | ●●● |
| Outdoors | ●●● |
| Observations | ●●○ (citizen science building; editorial notes today) |

Answers: what is happening this week, what to learn, where to pay attention, what to observe, how to go outside ethically.

---

### ForageCast — **Essential**

**Role:** Flagship application — habitat literacy, seasonal timing, ethical investigation.

| Pillar | Fit |
|--------|-----|
| Learn | ●●● |
| Outdoors | ●●● |
| Observations | ●●○ (foray/phenology via Fieldry + WOS) |

Position as **understanding why species appear where and when** — not “find mushrooms.”

---

### Fieldry — **Essential**

**Role:** Observation ledger and future WOS capture interface.

| Pillar | Fit |
|--------|-----|
| Learn | ●●● |
| Outdoors | ●●● |
| Observations | ●●● |

Highest pillar-3 potential. Consolidate journaling here; do not rebuild Field Notes tools in every app.

---

### Waypoint Scenes — **Important**

**Role:** Creative reflection from field evidence.

| Pillar | Fit |
|--------|-----|
| Learn | ●●○ |
| Outdoors | ●○○ (indirect) |
| Observations | ●○○ |

Ship v0.1.0 and freeze scope. Does not compete with Fieldry or ForageCast for platform priority.

---

## Demoted concepts

| Former product | Status | Absorption |
|----------------|--------|------------|
| Shed Hunting | Postpone | Winter wildlife content track |
| Steepleaf | Postpone | ForageCast botany & phenology track |
| SignalTerrain | Postpone | Shared map / navigation modules |
| Savant Sommelier | Postpone | Occasional terroir editorial |
| Terrainbound | Remove | Fieldry routes + Dashboard trail conditions |

---

## Overlap resolutions

| Overlap | Resolution |
|---------|------------|
| Species / phenology | One module; Steepleaf → ForageCast |
| Field journaling | Fieldry only |
| Maps / terrain | Shared `WDS.mapView`; no standalone map app |
| Seven rooms × 8 apps | Seven rooms = content pattern, not 8 repos |

---

## Investment sequence

1. Dashboard learning path + Pike County Preview honesty  
2. Fieldry MVP + WOS local capture  
3. ForageCast depth  
4. Scenes v0.1.0  
5. Content tracks (not app shells)
