# Version 1.0 — Emotional Roadmap

**North star:** *Bring Nature to Life.*

**Core idea:** *I have returned home from an amazing day outdoors.*

---

## The journey (not a feature list)

### 1. Arrival — Opening the studio
**Feeling:** Door closes. Pack drops. Quiet.

The welcome screen is not a landing page. It is the cabin. Warm light. No dashboard. One invitation: unpack a frame.

**1.0 bar:** Hero, copy, hearth light, and “Unpack” language ship everywhere. No “upload” in user-facing UI.

### 2. Unpacking — First frame on the easel
**Feeling:** Reliving the moment before editing it.

Import is ritual: choose one frame you love. Drag to easel. The studio wakes up locally. Nothing leaves the machine.

**1.0 bar:** Drag-drop, error messages in plain language, Living Scene opens automatically.

### 3. By the fire — Living Scene
**Feeling:** The day continues in the frame.

Fog breathes. Light shifts. Camera moves like someone watching, not performing. Moods (presets) read like weather through the window.

**1.0 bar:** Ten moods, calm defaults, atmosphere + observation sliders, gentle suggestions (“what we noticed”).

### 4. At the window — Parallax
**Feeling:** Presence. Depth. Stillness you can lean into.

Not weather — relationship between you and the frame. Pointer, touch, tilt when available.

**1.0 bar:** Five presence moods, hidden placeholder layers until true masks ship.

### 5. On the wall — Collections
**Feeling:** Today’s work, honored.

Featured frame large. Masonry gallery. Field notes. Captions. Location. One tap: bring to life or feel depth.

**1.0 bar:** Real portfolio images in `assets/Images/`, modal as leather journal.

### 6. Lamplight — Field Guide
**Feeling:** Curiosity for tomorrow’s outing.

Not documentation — observation, reflection, challenges. Every lesson ties studio work to the trail.

**1.0 bar:** Two tracks, field exercises + reflection + challenge per lesson.

### 7. Share — Export
**Feeling:** One still breath, sent outward.

Not a render farm — a single moment saved from a living frame.

**1.0 bar:** PNG export reliable. Video honestly “on the horizon.”

---

## Version 1.0 emotional acceptance criteria

| Studio | User should feel… |
|--------|-------------------|
| Welcome | Relief, arrival, privacy |
| Living Scene | Memory becoming alive |
| Parallax | Intimacy with depth |
| Collections | Pride in the day’s work |
| Field Guide | Pull toward the next outing |
| Export | Quiet satisfaction |

---

## After 1.0 (still emotional, not just technical)

- **True depth masks** — Parallax earns its name; illusion completes.
- **Video export** — Share a loop, not only a breath.
- **Real collections sync** — Your frames, not samples.
- **Seasonal moods** — Presets that follow the calendar of the natural world.

---

## What we will not ship in 1.0

- Feature sprawl for its own sake
- Gamification, streaks, or social feeds
- Cloud upload without explicit trust design
- Motion that competes with the photograph

**Ship calm. Ship timeless. Ship something worth hours by the fire.**
