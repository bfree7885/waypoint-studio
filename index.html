How Waypoint Scenes works

Portfolio, creative modes, and export tools — everything in one calm workspace.

Photography

Browse a calm portfolio of landscape, wildlife, and field work. Open any image to read captions, location, and gear notes — then send it to Living Scene or Interactive Parallax.

Living Scene

Upload a landscape and apply subtle camera drift with layered weather and atmosphere — fog, rain, snow, fireflies, and more. Start from a Scene Preset or tune every slider in Effects Studio.

Interactive Parallax

A separate 2.5D mode: your photo responds to mouse movement or phone tilt with layered translate and zoom. No weather overlays — just responsive depth. True subject masking arrives in a future update.

Export

Capture a snapshot of your living scene today. Video export, Live Photos, and desktop wallpapers are on the Waypoint roadmap.
