# Article / phenomenon topic — writer template

For weather events, geological processes, field skills, conservation stories, and research summaries.

**Type:** `phenomenon` | `skill` | `research` | `conservation`  
**Schema:** `topic-schema.json`

---

## Driving question

> _

---

## 1. What is it?

Open with an image, scene, or question — not a dictionary definition.

---

## 2. Why does it matter?

Stakes for the learner, place, or watershed. Cite research plainly if applicable.

---

## 3. How does it work?

Mechanism or technique. Use a diagram or numbered steps.

---

## 4. Where can it be found?

Geographic scope, landforms, or contexts where this appears. Link to OIP region when relevant.

---

## 5. When is it most relevant?

Season, diurnal pattern, storm cycle, or life-history timing.

---

## 6. How can someone safely observe it?

Safety · ethics · field prompts · mistakes to avoid.

---

## 7. How can they learn more?

Lessons · tools · companion video · weekend investigation link.

---

## 8. How does this connect to larger ecological systems?

Explicit web — what else had to be true for this to happen?

---

## Integrity

Label editorial vs verified research. Name sources. Use tentative language for forecasts.
