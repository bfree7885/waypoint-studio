# Waypoint Studio Constitution

## Mission

Observe. Understand. Create. Share.

Waypoint Studio exists to help people rediscover the natural world.

Technology is not the destination. Nature is.

## Purpose

Waypoint Studio builds digital field laboratories.

Every product should help people learn, go outside, observe, document, understand connections, create something meaningful, and optionally contribute to citizen science.

## Teaching Philosophy

Teach like a lab and field teacher.

The learning cycle is:

1. Observe
2. Ask questions
3. Learn
4. Use the tool
5. Test ideas outdoors
6. Reflect
7. Understand connections
8. Repeat

Every lesson should eventually lead people back outside.

## Every Product Must Include

- Home
- Learn
- Gallery
- Field Notes
- News
- Videos
- Tools

The tool is important, but the tool is not the whole product.

## Every Product Must Answer

- What can I learn?
- What can I do?
- How is this connected to everything else?
- How can I use this outdoors?

## Content Standards

Every product should feel like a modern field guide.

Use:

- beautiful photography
- illustrations
- diagrams
- maps
- articles
- videos
- species profiles
- field guides
- seasonal information
- outdoor challenges
- citizen science opportunities
- real stories
- research summaries

## Visual Style

Waypoint Studio should feel:

- warm
- cozy
- traditional
- handcrafted
- naturalist
- educational
- quiet
- timeless
- photography-first

It should NOT feel like:

- social media
- a startup dashboard
- enterprise software
- crypto
- gaming UI
- clickbait
- generic SaaS

## Citizen Science

Whenever appropriate, users may optionally contribute observations that support education, research, conservation, and ecological understanding.

Users must always understand:

- what data is collected
- why it matters
- how it may be used
- whether it is private, public, or anonymous

Never trick users into sharing data.

## Subscriptions and Income

Waypoint Studio can generate income through subscriptions, premium education, advanced tools, field guides, exports, and research-quality datasets.

But income supports the mission. It does not replace the mission.

People should subscribe because Waypoint Studio helps them become better observers of the natural world.

## Feature Test

Before adding any feature, ask:

Does this help someone:

- observe?
- understand?
- create?
- share?
- spend more time outdoors?
- learn something meaningful?
- understand connections in nature?

If not, do not build it yet.

## Final Rule

This Constitution is the highest authority for Waypoint Studio.

If future designs, AI suggestions, product plans, or features conflict with this document, this document wins.
